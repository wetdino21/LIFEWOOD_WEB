INTEGRITY

We are dedicated to act ethically and sustainably in everything we do. More than just the bare minimum, it is the basis of our existence as a company.

At Lifewood, we connect with the thousands of people who work with us in countries often described as 'developing economies' and we realise that we are being offered the chance to provide opportunities.

Opportunities for employment with wages high enough to change people's lives. Opportunities to teach and provide computing and tech-skills equal to anywhere in the world, making our people the breadwinners in their families.

For instance, in Bangladesh we employ a high proportion of women and even more surprisingly, the physically disabled. We have been especially inspired by the example of Tilotom, the eldest son of a low-income family. Physically disabled since birth, he's now the family's primary breadwinner, thanks to Lifewood's training and his determination to overcome all obstacles. He has quickly progressed to become one of the company's best performers, managing a large portfolio of responsibilities.

This is the Lifewood difference. Using AI and GPT we are constantly improving the way we go about our business, but we never lose sight of the truth, that what we do must have a human benefit for our people and our customers around the world.
